# User Guide - Shabaka Invest Group Administration

This guide explains how to manage the site content via the secure administrator dashboard.

---

## 1. Accessing Administration
To access the management panel:
1.  Go to the URL: `your-site.com/admin/login`
2.  Enter your administrator **Email** and **Password**.
3.  Click on "Login".

> **Note:** After 3 unsuccessful attempts, access may be temporarily blocked for security.

---

## 2. Dashboard
Once logged in, you arrive at the dashboard which displays:
- Total number of services, testimonials, and messages.
- The latest messages received via the contact form.
- A sidebar menu to navigate to different sections.

---

## 3. Content Management

### A. Managing Services (`/admin/services`)
This is where you define the offers visible on the site.
- **Add:** Click on "New Service". Fill in the name, short description (for preview), full description, and add an image.
- **Edit:** Click on the "Pencil" icon next to a service.
- **Delete:** Click on the "Trash" icon. **Warning, this action is permanent.**
- **Order:** You can reorganize the display of services by changing their order number.

### B. Managing Testimonials (`/admin/testimonials`)
Add reviews from your clients to strengthen credibility.
- **Rating:** From 1 to 5 stars.
- **Highlight:** Check "Highlight" to display this testimonial on the homepage.

### C. Managing Projects (`/admin/projects`)
Showcase your achievements.
- Each project can have an associated client, a category, and an external link.

---

## 4. Managing Contacts (`/admin/contacts`)
All messages sent via the site's contact form land here.
- **Reading:** Click on a message to view all details (Name, Email, Phone, Message).
- **Status:** An unread message appears in bold. It automatically switches to "Read" once opened.
- **Action:** You can reply directly by email by clicking on the sender's address.

---

## 5. Site Configuration (`/admin/settings`)
This section controls the global information of the site.
- **Identity:** Change the site name, logo, and favicon.
- **Contact:** Update the email, phone, and address displayed in the footer.
- **Social Networks:** Add or modify links to your Facebook, LinkedIn, etc. pages.
- **Scripts:** Add your tracking codes here (Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel) without touching the source code.

---

## 6. SEO Optimization (`/admin/seo`)
To improve your visibility on Google, configure each page:
- **Meta Title:** The blue title that appears in search results.
- **Meta Description:** The small gray text under the title.
- **Open Graph Image:** The image that displays when you share the link on WhatsApp or Facebook.

---

## 7. Media Library (`/admin/media`)
Manage all your image and document files.
- You can upload new images here to use later in your services or articles.
- Delete obsolete files to free up space.
